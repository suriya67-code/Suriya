Our History

Established in 1995, Islamia Public School began with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Islamic values and modern teaching methodologies. What started as a small institution with 50 students has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region, now serving over 2500 students from diverse backgrounds.

Over the years, we have consistently maintained our commitment to excellence, expanding our facilities, adopting innovative teaching methods, and producing outstanding graduates who have excelled in various fields nationally and internationally.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops intellectual, spiritual, emotional, and physical capabilities of students, preparing them to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Pakistan's leading educational institution that combines academic excellence with strong moral values, producing well-rounded individuals equipped to face the challenges of the 21st century.
